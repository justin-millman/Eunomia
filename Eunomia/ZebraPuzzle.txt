The Zebra Puzzle

Variously attributed to Albert Einstein and Louis Carroll, this logic puzzle appeared in a December issue ~Life International~ in 1962. It consists of thirteen total clues with a small number of clarifications, which have been reproduced on various websites (including Wikipedia, from which this version of the puzzle was reconstructed.)
